About

Who are we?

CSA Sports Management is a full-service sports management agency dedicated to providing clients and partners with assistance in contract negotiation, career + brand management services and financial planning with the utmost professionalism, reliability and transparency.

The name “CSA” represents the origins of the agency’s founders, each beginning their journeys in schools of basketball throughout Canada, Serbia and America (USA).

Services

CSA is proud to offer the following services to clients and partners to assist them in accomplishing their goals and establishing fulfilling careers.

Our team creates personalized strategic plans to serve as a guide to help our clients achieve their various career goals. Our number one priority is to maximize our clients’ professional and financial opportunities, providing assistance and guidance the entire way throughout each venture.

Contract Negotiation

We facilitate contract negotiation between professional basketball players, coaches and organizations

Financial Planning

Offer services in financial planning including budgeting, saving and investing to help create generational wealth for the families of our clients

Brand Management

Help maximize our clients’ personal brands and professional opportunities, providing assistance and guidance the entire way throughout each venture

Short + Long-Term Career Planning

Create custom plans to offer guidance and stability in the short and long term careers of our clients

Pre-Draft Preparation

Provide world class on and off court guidance in preparation for the NBA draft

Off-Season Training

Train with our partner pro skills trainers during the offseason to maximize any athletes game

Official Launch of CSA Sports Management

It is with great excitement we announce and celebrate the official opening of CSA Sports Management during the month of June! CSA Sports Management is a full-service sports management agency that is dedicated to providing clients and partners with assistance in contract negotiation, career + brand management services and financial planning with the utmost professionalism, reliability and transparency.

CSA Sports is proud to offer services to Professional Athletes, Coaches, Teams/Clubs and any other organizing bodies looking for guidance and/or assistance in their careers or sport-based events. Whether a rookie embarking on their first season or a veteran in their prime, CSA Sports works diligently to curate the best opportunities for all of our clients!

Our team of young, driven and reliable agents eager for success look for the same qualities when scouting potential clients. With operations based in Toronto, Canada; Belgrade, Serbia; and Mesa, Arizona, our network is spread across the globe and ever-growing. CSA will look to capitalize during the first year of operation and continue to strive to become a global leader in sports!
